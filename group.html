Stray kids

- is a South Korean boy group

formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017 through the survival show of the same name, Stray Kids. The group debuted on March 25, 2018, with the extended play (EP) I Am NOT and has since gained global recognition for their powerful performances, self-produced music, and unique sound.
